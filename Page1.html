Solar Energy

Using solar panels

Solar energy is a renewable source, in which light or energy from the sun is transformed into electrical energy. This has become a solution towards being more energy efficient. There are many benefits that come with using solar energy, which is why we have chosen to use this source of energy for our home in Oahu, Hawaii. We plan to be completely eco-friendly when it comes to the construction of the house, as we know that it can be profitable in many ways. In order to harness the energy from the sun, we must use solar panels. In particular we plan to use photovoltaic solar panels, which convert sunlight directly into electricity that can be used to power household appliances and equipment. These would be installed on the roof of the house, and for further design, cladding the roof with solar shingles would be a great idea considering that they are becoming more affordable. So, what are the benefits? Well, first of all "using solar panels will automatically lower the amount you spend on electricity up to 50%" according to “Home Self”. This is due to the fact that the electricity in your home will not be produced by burning non-renewable resources such as natural gas, and other fossil fuels. Apart from saving money, this will certainly affect the environment in the best possible way.
