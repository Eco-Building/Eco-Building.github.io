Geothermal Energy

An affordable source of energy

As we were analyzing some possible options for incorporating renewable sources into our home, we came across geothermal energy. This source of energy can be used to provide heating and cooling for the house, but it can also be used as a way to harness electricity. In particular, the two main factors that stuck out to us were the economic profit gained, as well as the environmental benefit. To begin with, including a geothermal system would lower the expenses of our clients, when it comes to having a source of energy, and it can possibly make you eligible for federal tax credits. According to “Kama’aina Plumbing and Renovations”, “while Geothermal heat pumps are more expensive to install, maintenance is less as they are installed underground and largely removed from the elements... This of course saves money and energy and reduces air pollution.”
